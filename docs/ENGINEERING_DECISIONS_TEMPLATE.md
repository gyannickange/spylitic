# Engineering Decisions Template

Use this to document non-trivial changes. Keep it short and practical.

## Title
Short name for the decision.

## Context
- What problem are we solving?
- Who is affected?
- What constraints exist?

## Decision
Describe the chosen approach. Keep it concise.

## Alternatives considered
- Option A: Why not?
- Option B: Why not?

## Consequences
- Positive outcomes.
- Risks or downsides.
- Follow-up work.

## Rollback plan
How to safely revert if needed.

## Tests and monitoring
- Tests added or updated.
- Metrics/logging changes.
